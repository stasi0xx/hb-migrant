Design a mobile-first weekly menu page for Głodny Niedźwiedź — Polish office catering. This is the product browsing page where users pick meals and add them to cart.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Device: Mobile (390px wide)
- Primary sections: deep bottle green #1B4332 background
- Accent/CTA: salmon pink #E8927C
- Secondary sections: warm cream #FDF6EC background
- Badges/categories: mustard yellow #D4A017
- Text on dark: off-white #F9F6F0
- Text on light: deep charcoal #1A1A1A
- Buttons: pill-shaped (rounded-full), bold, large
- Primary CTA: salmon pink background, white text
- Cards: rounded-2xl, cream, subtle shadow
- Typography: bold expressive headlines (Inter/DM Sans style)
- Style: premium food brand, warm and appetite-inducing

**Page Structure:**
1. Sticky nav: bottle green, back arrow left, "Menu tygodnia" title, cart icon with badge right
2. Week date header (cream): "20–24 marca 2025" with decorative bear paw accent
3. Category filter pills (horizontal scroll, cream bg): All | Dania główne | Wegetariańskie | Ryby | Zupy | Sałatki — active pill in salmon pink
4. Food item cards (cream bg, stacked): large food photo, name in bold dark, category badge mustard, weight/portion info, original price crossed out, discounted price (5% online discount) in bold salmon, "Dodaj do koszyka" button pill-shaped salmon
5. Floating cart summary bar (fixed bottom, bottle green): "🛒 3 dania · 78,45 zł" with "Przejdź do kasy →" button in salmon pink
6. Include at least 6 food cards: schabowy, łosoś, bowl wege, żurek, sałatka cezar, sushi

Make it feel like a premium food delivery app — think Deliveroo/Uber Eats level quality but with Głodny Niedźwiedź's warm green/salmon brand identity.
